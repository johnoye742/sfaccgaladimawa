about us

Who are we?

We are a community of God's people.
We are a united and holy family.

Where to find us?

Our church is located at Off Airport Road, Opp. Zimbabwe, Galadimawa, Abuja, Nigeria.

What are we?

We have various Pius Societies in our church. They are:

  • Catholic Alter Servers Association of Nigeria (CASAN)
  • Catholic Choir Association of Nigeria
