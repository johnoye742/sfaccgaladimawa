St. Francis Of Assisi Pastoral Area Galadimawa introuced 8 couples on Sunday, 29th of July, 2021

The Pastoral Area solemnize the marriage of Eight (8) couples in her effort to draw more families to the Eucharist by encouraging them to overcome the various impediments that had hitherto prevented them from approaching the Table of The Lord. These couples were preceded by another four (4) that solemnize theirs on 5th of May, 2021. Bringing them to a total of twelve (12) couples in this year alone and still counting. It is expected that there may be more since another marriage course is scheduled to start early September.
